CHS Galilean Fellowship



Silliman University Church


November 19, 2024

Role: Participant



  An event required for our CHS1 subject. Originally planned to be held in front of the Silliman Church but the heavy rain throughout the whole day prevented that from happening, so it was transferred to the 4th floor of Ausejo Hall. The sudden downpour was not anticipated and even our professor said there was no chance for rain which probably jinxed it. Anyway the event started with a trust test in which we students each took a turn to place our hand inside a bag without knowing what was inside. Then we portrayed the David and Goliath story with a lecture and afterwards were given bread for snacks. Due to this event, I had learned not to be too sure of the future due to present evidence. The rain was probably jinxed because a lot of people were sure it would not rain at all.
