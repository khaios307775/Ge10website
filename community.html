John Paul Casiwan & Dollen Gabutan


October 26, 2024

Role: Lead Organizer



  A stepping stone for the goal of helping out the community. This was the first community event I had the opportunity to organize, and it was a rewarding experience. There were no papers to organize this event as it was simply voluntary, We believed that what mattered was the action but we did email the supervisor of Richwood homes giving the green light to organize the event. Together with Ms. Dollen Gabutan, we aimed to bring joy to the children in our area through a feeding program and gift-giving activity. The event was held right outside our house, creating a welcoming and familiar space for the children to gather and celebrate. Given the timing of the event during the Halloween season, we added a festive touch by encouraging the kids to come dressed in costumes, which brought vibrant energy and excitement to the occasion.


  We extended beyond just our immediate neighborhood. Children from both inside and outside Richwood Homes were invited, and it was heartening to see so many little ones eager to participate. The event was more than just distributing food and gifts; it was a chance to create a sense of community and belonging among the children. To make the day even more enjoyable, we organized party games for the children to have fun. The smiles on their faces were the greatest rewards for all the effort we put into hosting the event.


  As I recalled making it my goal to help out the community after being part of the orphan outreach, I am planning on hosting more community events in the future.


  Because of this event, I will continue on my path of helping people out in the future. Seeing the children being shy at first and having fun during the games, I will probably plan on hosting a similar event in the future when I am financially stable.
